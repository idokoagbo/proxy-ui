Capabilities

Built for how logistics actually works

📡
Multi-channel monitoring
Email, WhatsApp, and inbound calls monitored simultaneously. One exception source across all channels — no more missed messages.
🎯
Smart recipient routing
Certin knows who needs to know based on the exception type. Breakdown? Driver, ops manager, insurance client — all get the right message on the right channel.
📞
Voice exception handling
Drivers call a service number instead of the dispatcher. AI answers, transcribes, classifies. She never picks up — just approves the draft on WhatsApp.
🔒
Human always in the loop
Nothing sends without dispatcher approval. Every action is logged — what was drafted, what was sent, who approved it. Full accountability.
